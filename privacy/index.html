Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 15, 2026

LyriFlow is a translation utility built on a "Transient-Processing" architecture. Your privacy is protected by technical design.

1. Data Minimization

LyriFlow processes all user inputs in the device's volatile memory. We do not transmit user inputs to our servers for storage. We collect zero persistent personal data.

2. AI Transparency

Translations are performed via AI. No user input is used to train our AI models. We do not engage in automated profiling.

3. Contact

For inquiries, contact the developer via the app's support email.
